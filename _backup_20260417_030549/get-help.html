Frequently asked questions

What if I do not know what kind of help I need?

That is okay. Start with 988 or the SAMHSA helpline and tell them what is happening. You do not need to have the perfect words.

Can I get help for someone else?

Yes. Families, friends, and caregivers can also call 988 or the SAMHSA helpline for guidance.

Is there recovery help near Anderson, Indiana?

Yes. Anderson and Madison County residents can use statewide treatment directories, local support listings, and crisis services to find options close to home.

What is naloxone?

Naloxone is a medication that can reverse an opioid overdose and save a life when given in time.
